Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Welcome to RoomFindR. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provides directly to us, such as when you create an account, search for properties, or communicate with owners. This includes:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location data (to help you find nearby properties).
  • Profile photos and property preferences.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process your bookings and facilitate communication between customers and owners.
  • Send notifications regarding your account or bookings.

3. Location Data

RoomFindR requests access to your location to provide search results based on your current vicinity. Location data is only collected while the app is in use and is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can view and update your profile information within the app. You may also request account deletion by contacting our support team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@roomfindr.com
